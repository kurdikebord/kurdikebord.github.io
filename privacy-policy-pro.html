Clauses

By using our Application You don't have to be worried because we can prove that all of your personal information consistent with applicable data protection law are preotected.

1. General clauses

1.1. The app does not request the network permission, and hence does not have the capability of making any network requests on its own. Your typing data, voice recordings, are never shared by the app.


1.2. If the app crashes, you may get the option to share a crash report via e-mail. If you accept, your device information and crash details will be shared with us for the purposes of investigating the crash and improving the app. Note that the crash details may include personal dictionary or typing data - you can always manually review the file before sending if in doubt.


1.3. You may choose to access certain external features such as downloading dictionaries, models, or checking for updates. This will be opened in your web browser.


2. Google Play specific clauses

2.1. You may be subject to Google's privacy policy.
