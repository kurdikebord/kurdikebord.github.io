Goran Gharib the copyright holder of Kurdish Keyboard (تەختەکلیلی کوردی) Both Free and PRO version, Provides this ( "Privacy Policy") to help you understand how our application works and which permissions is used in Android Manifest file, You may need to know how we may obtain as Kurdish Keyboard is used, This privacy policy may change in the future, So please check back with us periodically.

1. General clauses

1.1. The app does not request the network permission, and hence does not have the capability of making any network requests on its own. Your typing data, voice recordings, are never shared by the app.

1.2. If the app crashes, you may get the option to share a crash report via e-mail. If you accept, your device information and crash details will be shared with us for the purposes of investigating the crash and improving the app. Note that the crash details may include personal dictionary or typing data - you can always manually review the file before sending if in doubt.

1.3. You may choose to access certain external features such as downloading dictionaries, models, or checking for updates. This will be opened in your web browser.

2. Google Play specific clauses

2.1. You may be subject to Google's privacy policy.


Permission

The bellow info shows to you that which permission USED in the AndroidManifest.xml file by Kurdish Keyboard.

  • record audio: is USED to internall voice search feature that works without internet connection.
  • uses-permission android:name="android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO"
  • show notification: is USED to show a notification when the app crashed.
  • uses-permission android:name="android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS"
  • control vabiration: is USED to vibrate if the user has key press vibration enabled.
  • uses-permission android:name="android.permission.VIBRATE"

Other app capabilites

  • uses-permission android:name="android.permission.RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED"
  • uses-permission android:name="android.permission.WAKE_LOCK"
  • uses-permission android:name="android.permission.FOREGROUND_SERVICE"
