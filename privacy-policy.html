Privacy Policy

Last Update: April 2026

Goran Gharib, the copyright holder of Kurdish Keyboard (KurdiKebord | تەختەکلیلی کوردی), provides this "Privacy Policy" to help you understand how our application works and which permissions are used. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect new features or regulatory changes.

1. General Clauses

1.1. The app requests network access solely for functional purposes such as checking for app updates and downloading language resources. Your private typing data and voice recordings are never transmitted over the network.

1.2. If the app crashes, you may choose to share a report via e-mail. This report helps us investigate and improve the app. You can manually review the crash log before sending it.

1.3. Certain features like dictionary downloads or update checks require internet access to connect to our official servers. No personal data is shared during these connections.

2. Google Play Specific Clauses

Usage of the app is also subject to Google's Privacy Policy regarding license verification and Play Store interactions.

3. Permissions & Data Usage

We DO NOT collect or share personal information (e.g., name, location, email, device ID, or contacts).
We DO NOT transfer your personal typing data from your device to any third party.

POST_NOTIFICATIONS

Used to notify you when a new app update is available or if an error occurs, ensuring you have the latest features and security fixes.

INTERNET

Used to check for app updates from our official server, download dictionary files (.dict) for auto-correction, and fetch optional emoji fonts (.ttf).

RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED

Used to automatically reschedule periodic update checks and ensure keyboard services are ready after your device restarts.

VIBRATE

Used to provide haptic feedback (vibration) while typing, if enabled in settings.

READ/WRITE_USER_DICTIONARY

Used locally to learn your typing style and save new words you add for better predictions. This data remains strictly on your device.

4. Other App Capabilities

FOREGROUND_SERVICE & WAKE_LOCK

Used to ensure background tasks like dictionary downloads or update checks are completed without interruption.

CHECK_LICENSE

Used to verify the app's purchase status via the Google Play Store.
