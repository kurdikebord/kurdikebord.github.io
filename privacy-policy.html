"Personal Information" includes information that alone or when in combination with other information may be used to readily identify, contact, or locate you or a specific individual.

Collected information and usage

By using Kurdish Keyboard, you consent to the collection and use of your Personal Information by Kurdish Keyboard consistent with applicable data protection law and this Privacy Policy. We ARE NOT sharing Personal Information (e.g., name, address, telephone number, email address, location, device ID, contacts and personal device’s user-dictionary), and we ARE NOT transferring this Personal Information from your device, or Kurdish Keyboard's storage, to any third party or external device. The Keyboard collects and uses information from you in several ways:

  • Typing behavior is tracked and used to learn new words you use that are not in Kurdish Keyboard pre-built words dictionary. This Personal Information is stored on the device and is not transferred by the Keyboard to any other device or third party.
  • Typing behavior is also tracked and used to predict which words you commonly use after a other words (bi-grams).
  • Your contacts list is read to help Keyboard predict typing and correct typing. You may disabled this feature in the Setup process of Kurdish Keyboard or in the setting pages.
