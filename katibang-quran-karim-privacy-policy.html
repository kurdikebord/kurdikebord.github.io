Katibang Quran Al Karim

Last Update: June 2026

Goran Gharib, the copyright holder of Katibang Quran Al Karim (کاتی بانگ | قورئانی پیرۆز), provides this "Privacy Policy" to help you understand how our application works and which permissions are used. We may update this policy from time to time, so please check back periodically.

Used Permissions

INTERNET

Required to work the app to play audio files online. The app also uses Wifi & network permission as required for the functionality of the app.

ACCESS_WIFI_STATE

Used to check network connectivity for streaming audio content and downloading updates.

ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE

Used to determine network availability and connection type for optimal audio streaming.

Other App Capabilities

FOREGROUND_SERVICE

Used to continue playing audio in the background while using other apps.

RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED

Used to reschedule background services after device restart.

WAKE_LOCK

Used to keep the device awake during audio playback to prevent interruptions.

CHECK_LICENSE

Used to verify the app's purchase status via the Google Play Store.

Personal Data

We DO NOT share personal information (e.g. name, address, telephone number, email address, location, device ID, contacts and personal device's user-dictionary).
We DO NOT transfer this Personal Information from your device or Quran Al Karim's storage to any third party or external device.
