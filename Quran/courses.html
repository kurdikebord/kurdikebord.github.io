QURAN WITH TAJWEED

Quran reading with tajweed class for those students who already completed their Qaida Class or having some basic knowledge of Arabic. In this course,the student will add beauty to their recitation of the Quran. Quran recitation with Tajweed means to recite and pronounce each letter of the Quran which fulfills the right of that word. For that purpose, you should have a professional Quran Qari to help you with how to read Quran with Tajweed. Teaching tajweed requires a person to have a command over the Arabic language. That is why we only hire those teachers who are experts in the Arabic language and know its grammar in depth. We always recruit the best Quran teachers who help our students to learn Tajweed online We know that an incompetent teacher can never proficiently teach them to learn Tajweed Quran. QIRAAT COURSE

Learn Qirats online with highly-qualified Quran teachers through one-to-one online Quran classes. At our platform, we avail interested students the opportunity to take their Quran recitation skills to the next level by studying the 10 methods of Quran recitation based on the 10 recognized schools of Qira’at. Each one derives its name from the famous scholars of Qiraat. Each Qira’a has its own set of recitation & tajweed rules which may change the pronunciation method of the word entirely. We not only help students in learning Quran online but develop their interest in understanding the process of Quran learning in complete depth.
